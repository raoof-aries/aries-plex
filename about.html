WHO WE ARE

About us

Aries Group began its journey with its flagship venture, Aries Marine, and has since evolved into one of the most prominent multinational conglomerates in the Middle East. Over the past 17 years, the group has expanded its footprint significantly, now comprising 27 Years 79 companies across 29 countries. With a reputation for excellence and innovation, Aries Group has successfully collaborated with over 11,972 national and international clients, delivering more than 240,500 projects across diverse industries.

Building on this legacy of trust and performance, Aries Group launched a new venture: Aries Plex Private Limited - to establish a strong foothold in the entertainment landscape. Incorporated in September 2014, Aries Plex was established with the vision of creating a premier chain of multiplexes and entertainment hubs, dedicated to delivering unmatched cinematic experiences with cutting-edge technology and world-class amenities.

OUR JOURNEY

Aries Plex: A New Era in Entertainment

The first Aries Plex project was initiated in Thiruvananthapuram in December 2014, marking the beginning of a new chapter in cinematic experiences. The initial phase saw the launch of three state-of-the-art screens in June 2015, with an additional three screens opened in April 2016.

In March 2025, Aries Plex expanded its entertainment reach with a brand-new, three-screen multiplex housed within the Aries Complex in Punalur. By leveraging state-of-the-art infrastructure and cutting-edge technology, the company underscores our commitment to bringing world-class cinematic experiences closer to regional audiences, blending cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design.
